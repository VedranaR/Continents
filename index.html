EUROPE

Europe is densely populated

It's the third continent in the world, with population of around 739 millions of people. And it's divided into 50 sovereign states and 6 more states with limited recognition.

FRANCE

French cuisine is renowned for being one of the finest in the world. And the Eiffel Tower is one of its most popular tourist sites.

UNITED KINGDOM

The United Kingdom includes the island of Great Britain, the north-eastern part of the island of Ireland and many smaller islands. It is a monarchy, its queen being Elizabeth II since 1952.

GREECE

Greece is the birthplace of democracy and Olympic Games. It has so many islands that its coastline is the 11th longest in the world.
